About FC Barcelona

Founded in 1899, FC Barcelona is one of the most historic and successful football clubs in the world. Known for their unique style of play, often called "tiki-taka," and their famous youth academy, La Masia, Barcelona has produced some of the greatest players in football history, including Lionel Messi, Xavi Hernandez, and Andrés Iniesta.

The club is based in Barcelona, Catalonia, Spain, and plays its home matches at the Camp Nou stadium. Beyond football, the club also has professional teams in basketball, handball, and other sports.

FC Barcelona is more than a club — it's a symbol of Catalan culture and identity.
